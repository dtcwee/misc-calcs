If you add minutes to your timesheet every day, you will get a flexi-day* after ...

*Flexi-time/day: Think of a time 'bank account' where extra time spent at work is deposited. After you have accrued the equivalent of a working day, (assumed to be 7.5 hours) you may arrange a 'flexi-day' off. Flexi-time is usually calculated as a running total on your timesheet.
